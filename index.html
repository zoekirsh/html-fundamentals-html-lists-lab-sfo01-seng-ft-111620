Ingredients:

  • 2 slices of bread
  • 4 slices of cheese
    • cheddar
    • mozzarella
    • pepper jack
  • 1 tbsp of butter

Directions:

  1. Spread butter liberally on both sides of bread slices
  2. Place one slice in frying pan on medium head, and fry for 2 minutes until a golden crust begins to form
  3. Heap all the cheese onto the slice of bread in the pan, and cover with second slice of bread
  4. Gently turn over, and fry for another 5 minutes until cheese is melted and beginning to bubble
  5. Enjoy!
